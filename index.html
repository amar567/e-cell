ABOUT US

We, the Entrepreneurship cell of IISER Kolkata , is a team that began with a motive to provide a platform to the Entrepreneurial minds and bring potential changes. We continuously strive to let the creative minds to be entrepreneurial in every work they do. We hope to enable them to solve global challenges as we see students as the most magnificent capital, and intend to nurture them, provide them with opportunities for excellence. We conduct talks, invite budding entrepreneurs, and conduct workshops to enable smooth interaction between students, faculty, aspiring, and existing entrepreneurs.
